Privacy Policy for 1List

Last updated: June 22, 2026

1List ("the app") is designed to keep your data private. This policy explains what information the app does and does not handle.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. 1List has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. The app does not connect to any server and does not transmit your information anywhere.

Where Your Data Lives

Everything you create in 1List — your tasks and app settings — is stored only on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or to any third party. If you delete the app, this data is removed with it.

Third Parties

1List does not include any third-party software development kits, analytics providers, or advertising networks. No data is shared with anyone.

Children's Privacy

Because 1List collects no data, it does not knowingly collect information from children or anyone else.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
